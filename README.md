# EDA-Pizza-Sales-Analysis
## 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis
## Project Overview

This project analyzes a pizza restaurant's sales data to uncover business insights using Python for data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and data visualization.

The analysis answers key business questions such as identifying the best-selling pizzas, peak sales periods, monthly sales trends, and overall sales performance. The findings can help support menu optimization, inventory planning, and marketing decisions.

## Objectives

The project aims to answer the following questions:

1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. What is the total quantity of pizzas sold?
3. How many customer orders were placed?
4. How many pizza types are available?
5. What is the average pizza price?
6. During which hours are sales highest?
7. Which day of the week generates the most sales?
8. What are the top five best-selling pizzas?
9. How do sales change from month to month?
10. Which pizza types perform poorly and may require attention?
## Dataset

The dataset contains transactional records of pizza sales, including:

1. Order ID
2. Order Date
3. Order Time
4. Pizza Name
5. Pizza Category
6. Pizza Size
7. Quantity Ordered
8. Unit Price

## Key Findings

The restaurant generated $817,860.05 in total revenue.

Over 49,000 pizzas were sold.

Friday and Saturday recorded the highest sales.

Sales peaked during lunch and dinner hours.

The Classic Deluxe Pizza was the best-selling menu item.

Sales remained highly volatile throughout the year, with sharp seasonal spikes immediately followed by steep drops.
